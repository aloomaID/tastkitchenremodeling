Aberdeen, WA Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Aberdeen (/ˈæbərdiːn/) is a city in Grays Harbor County, Washington, United States. The population was 17,013 at the 2020 census. The city is the economic center of Grays Harbor County, bordering the cities of Hoquiam and Cosmopolis. Aberdeen is occasionally referred to as the 'Gateway to the Olympic Peninsula'.
