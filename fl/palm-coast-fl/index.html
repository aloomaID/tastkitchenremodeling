Palm Coast, FL Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Palm Coast is a city in Flagler County, Florida, United States. As of the 2020 United State Census, the population was 89,258, an increase of almost 300%, since the 2000 count of 32,832. The population was estimated to be 98,411 as of July 1, 2022. It is the most populous city in Flagler County.
